Web Dev Deja

aka Princess Shuri

I'm teaching myself Web Dev and Design. Since January, I've been taking The Complete Web Developers Course 2.0. I finished HTML and CSS section and I'm finishing up the JavaScript section. Next, I will learn JQuery, Bootstrap 4, Wordpress, PHP, MySQL, APIs, Mobile Apps, then Python. My goal as a Web Developer/Designer is to build websites for Artist, Writers, Non-Profits, Small Businesses and more!

I'm an Artist with a love for the Internet. Web Development and Design bridge the gap between the two similar fields. Since I started high school, I struggled to find a career path I will enjoy. I didn't want to be like my family who has jobs they hate. Why would I do that to myself? I want an exciting career which allows me to flex my creativity and my brain. Web Development and Design does that for me. This field is evolving fast and I have to keep learning new things every single day. I can bring peoples visions to life with a few lines of code.

That's why I keep learning.
